Red Beans and Rice

Description:

If you're looking for an introduction to the Cajun cuisine, then look no further.
Joining andouille sausage, fresh garden vegetables, spices, and your choice of rice. This authentic Louisiana dish packs a flavorful punch that will leave you salivating.
In this recipe you will learn everything needed to create this savory dish including the best sides to go along with it.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound dry red kidney beans
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 6 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspon Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 pound andouille sausage, sliced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups rice of choice

Steps:

  1. Rinse dry beans and soak in a large pot overnight
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat
  3. Cook down onion, bell pepper, celery and garlic for ~3-4 minutes
  4. Rinse dry beans again and transfer back to large pot with 6 cups water
  5. Mix in cooked vegetables with beans
  6. Add bay leaves, parsley, thyme, cajun seasoning and cayenne pepper
  7. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to medium-low, simmer for ~2 1/2 hours
  8. Brown sliced sausage in separate pan. Once browned, add to pot
  9. Continue simmering for another ~30 minutes
  10. While everything is simmering, add 4 cups chicken broth and 2 cups rice to a separate pot
  11. Bring chicken broth and rice to boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes
  12. Once all is cooked, serve beans over steamed rice
