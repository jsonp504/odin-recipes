Bacon, Egg and Cheese Sandwich

Description:

A bacon, egg, and cheese sandwich is a satisfying breakfast classic that combines crispy bacon, a fried or scrambled egg, and melted cheese between slices of bread
The bacon adds a smoky flavor, while the egg adds a hearty and protein-rich element. The melted cheese further enhances the deliciousness of the sandwich.
This classic breakfast sandwich is beloved for its simplicity, portability, and the delightful melding of savory ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices bacon
  • 4 teaspoons butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices bread

Steps:

  1. Place bacon in nonstick skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Cook until browned, turning occasionally
  3. Drain bacon slices on paper towel and wash out skillet (Optional:) Keep bacon grease for future use
  4. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper in a small bowl
  5. Melt 2 teaspoons butter in skillet over medium heat
  6. Add eggs and cook, lifting the edges so uncooked eggs run under the cooked side
  7. Shake and tilt skillet to move the uncooked egg and repeat until the top is set
  8. Reduce heat to low
  9. Chop bacon roughly and add to eggs. Add cheese and allow to melt
  10. Butter 2 slices of bread with 1/2 teaspoon butter each. Lay one slice bread, buttered side up over eggs
  11. Carefully turn egg over so buttered side of bread is on skillet
  12. Cook until bread is nice and golden brown
  13. Add second piece of bread and flip
  14. Cook until bread is golden brown
  15. Serve and enjoy!
